Welcome to Jesus Is The Answer – Community-Driven Center

Our mission is to instill hope, provide relief, and help restore one life at a time, to the glory of God.

Jesus Is The Answer – Community-Driven Center is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization serving families throughout our community with compassion, dignity, and practical support.

Our Impact

  • ❤️ Over 200 families served every week
  • 🥕 Fresh produce and pantry staples distributed weekly
  • 🤝 Volunteers and community partners working together
  • 🏠 Serving the Jamaica, Queens community

Stories of Hope

Every Tuesday, our doors open to welcome over 200 families seeking food assistance and encouragement. Through the generosity of donors, volunteers, churches, and community partners, families receive fresh produce, pantry staples, and other essential items in a welcoming environment.

Together, we are building stronger communities through compassion, resources, and opportunity.

Weekly Community Food Pantry

Every Tuesday | 12:00 PM – 2:00 PM

Our food pantry is open to the public every Tuesday. Fresh groceries and essential food items are available while supplies last. No appointment is required. Everyone is welcome.

Partner With Us

We welcome partnerships with wholesalers, manufacturers, churches, businesses, volunteers, and community organizations.
